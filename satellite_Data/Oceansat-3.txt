OCEANSAT-3 (EOS-06)
OCEANSAT-3, also known as EOS-06 (Earth Observing Satellite-06), is a satellite mission designed to continue and enhance the operational services of the Oceansat-1, Oceansat-2, and SCATSAT-1 missions. It offers a unique capability for simultaneously measuring physical, biological, and atmospheric parameters using its onboard instruments.

OCEANSAT-3 is equipped with four primary instruments:

Ocean Colour Monitor (OCM-3): A 13-band advanced imager developed by ISRO.

Scatterometer (SCAT-3): A Ku-band instrument developed by ISRO.

Sea Surface Temperature Monitor (SSTM): A 2-channel monitor developed by ISRO.

ARGOS: A data collection platform for various ocean-based systems, developed by CNES (French Space Agency).
