Oceansat-2 Satellite
Oceansat-2 is a satellite mission designed to continue and enhance the operational services of its predecessor, Oceansat-1 (IRS-P4). Its mainframe systems are derived from previous Indian Remote Sensing (IRS) missions.

Oceansat-2 carries three primary instruments:
